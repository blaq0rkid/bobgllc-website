## Step 1: Automating Requirements Verification

### Compliance Matrix automation
- **Extract requirements:** every “shall,” submission instruction, attachment, and certification.

This is the fastest way to convert “we think we’re compliant” into “we can prove we’re compliant.”

## Actionable Intelligence: The SAM.gov Saved Search “L/M + CUI + AI” Test
Go to SAM.gov and create a saved search for your target agency (VA, Navy, or DHA) using keywords: **Section L**, **Section M**, **CUI**, **NIST 800-171**, **CMMC**, and **AI**.

Open one active solicitation from the results. Pull Sections **L** and **M**. Then run your AI tool to list **every mandatory certification requirement** and **every “shall”** tied to submission.

If it misses one item or can’t cite the page/section, it’s not configured for GovCon compliance.
